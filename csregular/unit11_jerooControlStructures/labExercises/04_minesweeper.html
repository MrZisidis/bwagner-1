MineSweeper

Directions

A jeroo with 50 flowers in its pouch travels across an island one time from East to West disabling every net it finds in its path and replacing the net with a flower (first, toss a flower at the net to disable it, then hop forward and plant another flower to replace the net). The program should use a loop to continuously find and replace nets until the shore is reached. Your implementation should work for any island and from any starting location on the East shoreline.

Create any jeroo methods you find necessary.
