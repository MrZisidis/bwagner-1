Transpose

Directions

Create a 5 x 5 matrix filled with random numbers in the range of -100 to 100. Print the orginal matrix and its transpose with appropriate labels as shown.

The transpose of a two-dimensional array (matrix) is obtained by interchanging rows and columns. Thus, Row 0 of the original becomes Column 0 of the transpose, Row 1 of the original becomes Column 1 of the transpose, and so on.
