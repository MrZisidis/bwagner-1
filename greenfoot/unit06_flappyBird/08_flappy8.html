FlappyBird8

Download and Open Senario

  1. Click on the following link to download the senario: FlappyBird8

  2. Select Open.

  3. Copy the folder FlappyBird8 and paste it in your MyGreenfoot folder.

  4. Launch Greenfoot and select Open from the Senario menu.

  5. Navigate to your MyGreenfoot folder, select the FlappyBird8 senario, and click Open.
