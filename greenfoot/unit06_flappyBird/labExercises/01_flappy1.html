In this 8 part series we are going to write a version of the FlappyBird game. The FlappyBird game was a mobile-device game developed by a programmer in Vietnam in 2013. In January of 2014 it became the most downloaded free game on the IOS App Store, but in February of 2014 the developer took it down citing guilt over its addictive nature and overuse.

The game is a side-scroller where the player controls a bird, attempting to fly between rows of green pipes without coming into contact with them.

FlappyBird Class

To start our game we create two methods in the FlappyBird class named fly and displayGameOver. The fly method allows Flappy to move up and down vertically. The BOOST_SPEED constant provides lift so the bird goes upward(climbs) and as gravity(g) is applied the bird gradually goes downward(falls).

The displayGameOver method is left as an exercise.
