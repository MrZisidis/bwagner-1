Download and Open Senario

  1. Click on the following link to download the senario: FlappyBird1

  2. Select Open.

  3. Copy the folder FlappyBird1 and paste it in your MyGreenfoot folder.

  4. Launch Greenfoot and select Open from the Senario menu.

  5. Navigate to your MyGreenfoot folder, select the FlappyBird1 senario, and click Open.

In this 8 part series lesson we are going to write a version of the FlappyBird game. The FlappyBird game was a mobile-device game developed by a programmer in Vietnam in 2013. In January of 2014 it became the most downloaded free game on the IOS App Store, but in February of 2014 the developer took it down citing guilt over its addictive nature and overuse.

The game is a side-scroller where the player controls a bird, attempting to fly between rows of green pipes without coming into contact with them.
