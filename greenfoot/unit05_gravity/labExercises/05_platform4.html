Fish

In this lesson the game has been modified to include fish. The fish can swim across the screen from left to right or right to left. Once a fish swims all of the way across the screen it is removed from the world. When a fish is created by the world its location, direction, and speed are randomly determined. The fish themselves are also created at random. Each time the act method is called there is a 5% chance of a fish being added to the world.

Program Modifications
