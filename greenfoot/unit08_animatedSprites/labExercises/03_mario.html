In this lesson features of the AnimatedActor class are demonstrated by animating a Mario actor as he moves.

Mario Sprite Sheet

The mario sprite sheet consists of 20 frames. The first 10 frames are right facing and are numbered from 0 - 9. The second 10 frames are left facing and are numbered from 10 - 19.

Mario Class

The Mario class's movementTest method is responsible for allowing a Mario object to move horizontally within the world. As a mario moves it is also animated using the sprite sheet frames. When he is moving right only frames 0 through 9 are used and when he is moving left only frames 10 through 19 are used. The method movementTest is shown below. Study the code until you understand how it works.
